Thanks for stopping by! With a bachelor's degree in engineering, I pivoted myself to be a self-taught designer in 2014. I moved to New York City in 2020 after living and working in India & the UAE for six years. Travelling and photography are the fuel for my soul.

With a master's specialization from the School of Visual Arts(MFA Interaction Design 2022), I like tackling complex problems while collaborating with teams in:

Distilling user research into product requirements that are coherent with the business goals

Conceptualizing the product by creating wireframes and information architecture

Building a design system for UI to keep a consistent visual vocabulary in the product

Building hi-fi prototypes for elevator pitch and conduct usability test to iterate the product further
